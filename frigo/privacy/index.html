Privacy Policy for Frigo frigo does not collect any personal data! We use Google Analytics to help improve the product by gathering anonymous usage data such as: - How often the app is used - Which make/model of device the app is running on - How long the app is used for - How often the barcode scanner was able to retrieve a product We also use Google Analytics to gather insights in to food waste habits such as - What categories of food people waste the most - How much they waste in terms of quantity and frequency - The reason the food was wasted
